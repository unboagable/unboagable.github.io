MY HISTORY IN PHOTOGRAPHY

I used to hate being in photographs. Standing awkwardly looking at the shiny camera while trying to maintain a smile. But once your behind the camera it's a different story. It'll sound cliche and free spirityyy but it's such a great opportunity to frame your world looking for beauty.

I enjoy taking landscape photos, people in their happy unawares, studies on mundane objects or subtle concepts. But mostly just exploring and very new to photography.

Anyway enjoy photos of beautiful people and beautiful things!
