My Story

My journey in photography

I used to hate being in photographs. Standing awkwardly looking at the shiny camera while trying to maintain a smile. But once you're behind the camera it's a different story. It might sound clich√© and free-spirited, but it's such a great opportunity to frame your world looking for beauty.

I enjoy taking landscape photos, capturing people in their happy unaware moments, and studying mundane objects or subtle concepts. I'm mostly exploring and relatively new to photography, but I find joy in discovering the extraordinary in the ordinary.

Photography has taught me to see the world differently - to appreciate light, composition, and the stories that unfold around us every day. Each image is a moment frozen in time, a memory preserved, and a story waiting to be told.

Enjoy these photos of beautiful people and beautiful things!
